Can I use SC Mobile App while staying overseas?

SC Mobile App is easily accessible from anywhere you travel throughout the world, except in certain countries/areas that are subject to international economic sanctions.

We are committed to complying with those sanctions. As such, we do not allow our products and services to be used directly or indirectly in Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria, Crimea and Sevastopol.

Access to our products and services will continue to be blocked for as long as you are in Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria, Crimea and Sevastopol.

You can resume your use of these products and services when you are outside these countries/areas.

SC Mobile Key

Set Up the 6-digit PIN and Offline PIN

What is SC Mobile Key? 

SC Mobile Key is an enhanced feature for SC Mobile App that enables you to authenticate your digital banking login and transactions.

With the SC Mobile Key bound and enabled on your mobile phone / device, you can get things done easily without any SMS OTP and / or transaction token.   

What are the benefits of SC Mobile Key? 

Seamless: No longer will you need to key in an OTP to authenticate your banking transactions or log in to access Online Securities. Let SC Mobile Key gets it done for you. 

Secure: When performing high-risk transactions such as adding a new payee, SC Mobile Key will digitally authenticate them for you, protecting such transactions with an additional layer of security. 

Anywhere: Worried that your SC Mobile Key will not work when you are overseas? The truth is: your SC Mobile Key can work for you even when there is no data connectivity. In an offline mode, you can generate your Offline PIN and authenticate high-risk transactions by using a challenge response code. 

How do I activate SC Mobile Key? 

Download the latest SC Mobile App. Login with your SC Online Banking username and password or Face ID / Touch ID, and you will be prompted to bind and activate SC Mobile Key.  

Why do I need to create a 6-digit SC Mobile Key PIN? 

Your 6-digit SC Mobile Key PIN provides an additional layer of security when you authenticate high-risk transactions (add payee, limit maintenance, update personal information, and etc.) or when you want to generate an Offline PIN. You are the only person with knowledge of this 6-digit PIN and who can use it to authenticate such transactions. 

Do I have to reset my SC Mobile Key when I change my mobile phone / device? 

Yes, your SC Mobile Key can be bound to only one mobile phone / device. If you want to transfer your SC Mobile Key to a new mobile phone / device, please re-activate it. 

I forgot my 6-digit SC Mobile Key PIN. What should I do? 

You can reset it with your SC Mobile App: 

Step 1: After logging in to SC Mobile App, select “Services” in the bottom menu. 

Step 2: In Settings, tap “Change SC Mobile Key” > “Change PIN” 

Step 3: Enter the OTP sent to you via email. 

Step 4: Enter the OTP sent to you via SMS. 

Step 5: Create your 6-digit PIN 

Step 6: Your PIN is successfully reset. Now you can authenticate all transactions with your new 6-digit PIN. 

How can I change my 6-digit SC Mobile Key? 

You can reset it with your SC Mobile App: 

Step 1: After logging in to SC Mobile App, select “Services” in the bottom menu. 

Step 2: In Settings, tap “Change SC Mobile Key” > “Change PIN” 

Step 3: Enter the OTP sent to you via email. 

Step 4: Enter the OTP sent to you via SMS. 

Step 5: Create your 6-digit PIN 

Step 6: Your PIN is successfully reset. Now you can authenticate all transactions with your new 6-digit PIN. 

Can I activate SC Mobile Key on more than one mobile phone / device? 

No, for security reasons, you can only activate your SC Mobile Key on one mobile phone / device. If you want to activate your SC Mobile Key on another mobile phone / device, the old one on the original mobile phone / device will be disabled. 

I am using Online Banking / another device to login but I cannot receive push notification to authenticate my login. How can I get offline PIN? 

 

Step 1: In SC Mobile App login page, tap “Generate Offline PIN” > “Offline PIN for login” 

Step 2: Based on instruction, input 6-digit SC Mobile Key PIN, there will be a set of 6-digit offline PIN generated. 

Step 3: Input the 6-digital offline PIN in Online Banking / another device to authenticate your login. 

I am using Online Banking / another device to perform a transaction, but I cannot receive push notification to authenticate my transaction. How can I get offline PIN? 

 

Step 1: In SC Mobile App login page, tap “Generate Offline PIN” > “Offline PIN for transaction” 

Step 2: Based on instruction, input 6-digit SC Mobile Key PIN 

Step 3: Input the 8-digit code displayed in Online Banking / another device into your binded device. There will be a set of 6-digit offline PIN generated. 

Step 4: Input the 6-digital offline PIN in Online Banking / another device to authenticate your login.

Communication Preferences

How to Enable Push Notification and Change Preferences

What is the function of Push Notification?

As an enhancement to your SC Mobile App, you can receive push notifications for banking services (such as service messages, transaction alerts, offers and product information).

Included as well is a Notification Centre that will centrally store all your push notifications from Standard Chartered.

How can I enable Push Notification to receive the latest banking information?

You can enable Push Notification by either of the two following methods:

  1. After logging in to SC Mobile App, in the bottom menu, select “Services” > “”Communication Preference” > “Push Notification” > ensure the button to the right of “Push notification inbox” is switched to the right to activate the function.

  2. In SC Mobile App, tap the bell icon on the top right > at the button, press the “Turn on” button to activate Push Notification.
How can I disable Push Notification?

After logging in to SC Mobile App, in the bottom menu, select “Services” > ”Communication Preferences” > “Push Notification” > ensure the button to the right of “Push notification inbox” is switched to the right to disable the function.

What if I have disabled Push Notification for the SC Mobile App?

You will continue to receive information from us via SMS and email.

What if any push notifications fail to be delivered to my mobile phone / device?

You will receive that information via SMS and email.

What kind of banking alerts will be sent via push notifications?

You may receive push notifications for service messages, transaction alerts, offers and product information, such as Bill Payment, Cash Withdrawal, Fund Transfer, Retail Purchase and Cheque Return for your banking accounts, as well as credit card payment due date reminder.

Can I enable Push Notification on more than one mobile phone?

For security reasons, we only allow customers to enable Push Notification on one mobile phone.

If you want to enable this function on another mobile phone, your Push Notification on the original mobile phone will be automatically disabled.

What if I have lost my mobile phone?

Please contact us immediately for assistance with service suspension before you re-enable Push Notification on another mobile phone.

I bought a second-hand mobile phone. Why can’t I enable Push Notification on it?

That mobile phone most likely still has the previous customer’s SC Mobile App installed and Push Notification enabled.

You need to uninstall the existing SC Mobile App and download it again before you can enable Push Notification.

Where can I check push notifications in SC Mobile App?

After logging in to SC Mobile App, tap the ring bell on the top right > “Notification” to check push notifications.

Why are iPhone users suggested to open the SC Mobile App every 30 days to view push notifications?

iPhone users who have enabled the function are suggested to open their SC Mobile App every 30 days to check push notifications, which cannot be received when the app is not running in the background.

I am an Android user. Why am I not able to receive push notifications when in China?

Google Play is prohibited in mainland China. Therefore Android mobile phones / devices are unable to receive push notifications in mainland China.

How do I choose the channels to receive communications relating to offers and product information?

After logging in to SC Mobile App, in the bottom menu, select “Services” > ”Communication Preferences” > “Marketing Offers & Product Updates” to choose among various channels to receive communications relating to offers and product information.

Will I receive push notifications every time I spend with my credit card(s)? Can I stop receiving such notifications?

When you carry out Card-Not-Present transactions (i.e. transactions that are completed without physical presentation of the relevant credit card, including but not limited to online transactions, mail / telephone orders), we will send you push notifications, which cannot be disabled. Such alerts are meant to avoid the risk of any unauthorized use of your credit card(s) and to help you easily keep track of your daily banking.

Can I stop receiving marketing promotions?

Yes, you can. After logging in to SC Mobile App, in the bottom menu, select “Services> ”Communication Preferences” > “Marketing Offers & Product Updates” to change the settings.

Pay & Transfer

Fund Transfers, Bill Payments, Add Payees, Digital Wallets

Where can I set my Daily Transfer Limits? Why are my Daily Transfer Limits preset / reset at HKD0?

To update your Daily Transfer Limits, please log in to SC Mobile App, select “Pay & Transfer” > “Manage” > “Daily Limit Maintenance”.

For online security and protection of your bank accounts, the daily online transfer limits for newly registered SC Online Banking and SC Mobile App customers are preset at HKD0 (but previously set transfer limits will remain unchanged after any software upgrade).

Besides, if you have not made any transfer for a particular transfer type (such as Third Party Local Transfers) via SC Online Banking of SC Mobile App for a period of time, the transfer limit for such transfer type will be reset to HKD0.

Do I have to pay any charges for making a transfer with a credit card?

From now till 30 December 2023 (both dates inclusive), the 3.5% over-limit electronic fund transfer handling fee will be waived for an accumulated transferred amount of up to HKD80,000 made by a Cardholder in a calendar month (based on the transaction date) when using SC Mobile App or SC Online Banking  to make any local transfer, bill payment, digital wallet top up transaction from such Cardholder’s Standard Chartered / MANHATTAN Credit Card(s).

3.5% handling fee will apply to any transferred amount exceeding HKD80,000 and will be debited from the valid credit card account with the highest transferred amount. If the last day of a calendar month is a Sunday, any related transactions made on that day will be counted in the following calendar month. The credit card account should be valid and with sufficient available credit limit during the transfer. 3.5% handling fee will apply to a transferred amount of above HKD25,000 and charges will be debited from the valid credit card account with the highest transferred amount if the total accumulated transferred amount exceeds HKD25,000 by a Cardholder in a calendar month (based on the transaction date) when using electronic banking services, person to person (P2P) payment services or mobile device / app / electronic funds transfer platforms, effective from time-to-time, to make any transfer / top up transaction from such Cardholder’s Standard Chartered / MANHATTAN Credit Card(s) to a specified account (including but not limited to Octopus Wallet account).

If the last day of a calendar month is a Sunday, any related transactions made on that day will be counted in the following calendar month.

Any related transactions made on the first day of a calendar month and before or during the relevant system update, will be counted in the preceding calendar month.

When a Cardholder is making an electronic fund transfer transaction and the accumulated transferred amount exceeds the cap, a message will be prompted at SC Online Banking / SC Mobile App (as the case may be) to indicate the transfer amount applicable for the handling fee waiver and the handling fee which may be incurred.

By confirming to proceed with the transfer, the Cardholder will be deemed to have agreed to pay the handling fee (where applicable), as shown by SC Online Banking / SC Mobile App, and prescribed by the Bank from time to time under the terms and conditions stated herein and by other promotions.  

Please refer to the latest Terms and Conditions for the Over-limit Electronic Fund Transfer Handling Fee Promotion Offer.

What are the differences between Local Transfer and SC Pay (FPS)?
To make a Local Transfer, you need to enter the bank account number and the payee must be registered beforehand via SC Mobile App or SC Online Banking. It is subject to the daily transfer limit for third party accounts, which is HKD1,000,000.

Mobile number, email address or FPS ID is used for SC Pay and payee registration is not required. It is subject to the daily transfer limit for non-registered payees, which is HKD300,000.
What are the types of fund transfer available for Local Transfer?
For “Local Transfer”, funds can be transferred, only in HKD or RMB, to a registered third party bank account held with Standard Chartered or other local banks.
My credit card account(s) cannot be found on the list for choosing the debit account. Why?
If your payee has been registered by signed request (denoted by * in payee list), currently you may only select a deposits account to transfer from.
Why was my fund transfer rejected due to “payee name not matched”?
When processing the transfer instruction, the receiving institution may verify, among other things, the payee’s name and account number against its records.

To avoid rejection, please check with the payee for the full account name maintained with the receiving institution.

If necessary, please update by adding the payee again via SC Mobile App or SC Online Banking.
Why was my payee deleted?

A payee will be deleted if there is no record of successful transfer from any of your bank accounts with us, during the 12 months after such payee is added via SC Mobile App / SC Online Banking.

You may add the payee again if you want.

To add a local account / bill payee via SC Mobile App, is it the same as the way with SC Online Banking?

It’s the same. The payee list is also the same for SC Mobile App and SC Online Banking.

How to add a local account / bill payee in SC Mobile App?

You can go to SC Mobile App, select “Pay & Transfer” > “My Payees”, scroll to the bottom > “Add Payee” to add a payee.

Who can register for SC Pay (FPS)?
To register for SC Pay (FPS), you need to:
  • Be an SC Online Banking customer and a registered user for SC Mobile App;

  • Have a valid mobile number or email address registered with the Bank; and

  • Hold an eligible HKD or CNY sole or “either-to-sign” joint account (Current, Savings or Integrated Deposits Account).
I have an Integrated Deposits Account which supports both HKD and RMB. When I enter the transfer amount for SC Pay (FPS), why is it displayed only in HKD / RMB?

Please make sure that you have bound the Integrated Deposits Account to SC Pay as the registered account and have activated both the HKD and RMB accounts.

Can I make a fund transfer in RMB from my HKD account, or to a HKD account of my payee?

Currently, accounts registered with SC Pay are only allowed to make fund transfers in the same currency.

If the payee’s FPS default account is not an integrated account that supports both HKD and RMB, a fund transfer in RMB to his or her HKD account could be rejected.

What is the FPS default receiving bank? Will my payee know which bank is my FPS default receiving bank?

Unless your FPS payer has named a particular bank as the receiving bank, the funds transferred to you via FPS will be credited to your default receiving bank, and the name of the receiving bank will not be disclosed to the payer.

Can I use SC Mobile App to check my FPS registration with another bank? How do I change the FPS default receiving bank to Standard Chartered?

You can go to “Management” > “SC Pay (FPS) Registration and Settings” to check the name of the other bank which you have registered for FPS with your mobile number or email address and delete the record.

If you have not assigned Standard Chartered as your FPS default receiving bank, on the same screen, you may tap the option icon next to your mobile phone or email address, and select “SCB as default receiving bank”.

Can I use my credit card as the registered account for SC Pay to receive FPS payments?

You must use a Standard Chartered savings / current account as the registered account for SC Pay.

A credit card account can be used as the debit but not credit account for HKD payments.

When I select “Pay Bills & Credit Cards” > “SC Credit Card”, can I pay the Standard Chartered Credit Card of my family member or friend?

When you select “Pay Bills & Credit Cards” > “SC Credit Card”, you can only pay your own Standard Chartered Credit Card(s) by debiting your bank account.

If you want to pay your family member or friend’s credit card, please go to “Pay Bills” and “Add Bill Payee” with the relevant credit card number before making payment.

I found an FPS QR code for payment on the bill. Do I have to register first before using Scan & Pay?

Before using Scan & Pay, please log in to SC Mobile App to register for SC Pay with your mobile number and email address registered with us.

Does Scan & Pay work with the QR codes for other payment platforms?

What is the difference between “Transfer History” and “Scheduled Transfer”?

“Transfer History” shows the transfers and payments which have been executed, while “Scheduled Transfer” is a pending instruction.

We need both the front and back shots of your HKID to complete identity verification for you without a branch visit.

Make sure there is no shadow or glare cast over your HKID, and the image is clearly visible and not out of focus.

The above factors may make it impossible for the system to detect a valid HKID in order to complete the identity verification. In this case, please visit any of our branches for account opening.
